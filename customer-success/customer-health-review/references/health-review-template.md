# Customer Health Review Template

Use this as additional reference material when performing health reviews.

## Standard Health Dimensions

1. Product Adoption & Value Realization
2. Technical Health & Integration Stability
3. Relationship Strength & Executive Alignment
4. Support Experience
5. Renewal & Expansion Readiness

## Suggested Data Sources

- Product analytics / usage dashboards
- Support ticket system (Zendesk, Intercom, etc.)
- CRM notes (Salesforce, HubSpot)
- NPS / CSAT responses
- Renewal and expansion pipeline in CRM
- Executive sponsor mapping
